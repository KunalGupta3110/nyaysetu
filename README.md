# ⚖️ NyaySetu – AI Legal Assistant for India

**NyaySetu** is an AI-powered legal platform designed to simplify Indian law and make legal assistance accessible, understandable, and actionable for everyone.

---

## 🚀 Overview

NyaySetu bridges the gap between complex legal systems and everyday people by providing:

* Instant legal guidance
* AI-powered document generation
* Simplified explanations of laws
* Multilingual accessibility

---

## 💡 Core Capabilities

### 🤖 AI Legal Assistant

Get instant answers to legal queries in a clear and structured format tailored for Indian law.

---

### 📄 Smart Document Generator

Create legally structured documents within seconds:

* Legal Notice
* Affidavit
* Rent Agreement
* Consumer Complaint
* RTI Application
* Bail Application

Each document follows a **formal, court-ready structure**.

---

### 📚 Law Simplifier

Transforms complex legal language into easy-to-understand explanations, enabling better decision-making.

---

### 🌍 Multilingual Accessibility

NyaySetu is designed for India’s diversity with support for multiple regional languages, ensuring inclusivity across users.

---

### 🎤 Voice Interaction *(In Progress)*

Users will be able to interact with the platform using voice — making it accessible even for non-literate users.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To build a platform where **every individual in India can understand their rights and take informed legal action without barriers**.

---

## 🧠 Technology Stack

* Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
* Backend: Node.js + Express
* AI Engine: LLM-based (Groq API integration)
* Document Generation: jsPDF

---

## 🌐 Platform Access

NyaySetu is deployed and accessible via web.
