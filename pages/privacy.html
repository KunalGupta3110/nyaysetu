How NyaySetu handles your information

This policy explains what we collect, why we use it, and the choices you have when using NyaySetu.

Introduction Data Collection Data Usage Sharing User Rights Security Disclaimer

Introduction

NyaySetu is an AI-powered legal information platform. We aim to make legal guidance easier to understand, while respecting your privacy and keeping our data practices simple.

Data Collection

We may collect information you provide directly or through login features, including:

  • Name, email address, password, city, and selected role when you create an account.
  • Student details such as college, year of study, and area of interest if you join as a law student.
  • Google login details such as your name, email address, and profile picture when you choose Google sign-in.
  • Legal questions, document inputs, or text you submit to use AI features.
  • Basic usage data needed to improve reliability, performance, and safety.

In the current browser-based version, some account/session details may be stored in your browser using localStorage.

Data Usage

We use your information to provide the platform, personalize your dashboard, process AI requests, improve features, protect against misuse, and communicate important updates.

We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing

We may use trusted tools or service providers for authentication, hosting, translation, analytics, or AI processing. We only share what is needed for those services to work.

If a query is reviewed by a student or professional workflow, relevant query content may be visible to reviewers for quality and safety purposes.

User Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account information where technically possible. You can also clear locally stored browser data at any time from your browser settings.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no online system is completely secure. Please avoid submitting highly sensitive personal, financial, medical, or case-confidential details unless necessary.

No Legal Advice Disclaimer

NyaySetu provides legal information and AI-generated assistance. It is not a law firm and does not replace advice from a qualified advocate. You are responsible for how you use information from the platform.

For urgent, complex, or high-risk matters, consult a licensed legal professional before taking action.
